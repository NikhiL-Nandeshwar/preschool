Our Programs

Age-appropriate learning programs designed to nurture every childs growth.

Playgroup

(2 - 3 Years)

A gentle introduction to school life through play, music, movement, and social interaction in a safe and caring environment.

Our Playgroup program provides a warm and nurturing environment where young children begin their learning journey through play.
Activities include music, movement, storytelling, and hands-on exploration.
The program focuses on developing social skills, emotional security, and early motor development.
Children learn to interact, share, and build confidence at their own pace.

Nursery

(3 - 4 Years)

Structured learning combined with creative play to develop early literacy, numeracy, communication, and self-confidence.

The Nursery program combines structured learning with creative play to support early childhood development.
Children are introduced to basic literacy and numeracy through fun, engaging activities.
Emphasis is placed on language development, creativity, and social interaction.
This stage helps build independence, curiosity, and self-confidence.

Kindergarten Preparation

(4 - 5 Years)

Focused preparation for formal schooling with activity-based learning, problem-solving skills, and emotional readiness.

Our Kindergarten Preparation program is designed to prepare children for a smooth transition into formal schooling.
The curriculum includes early reading, writing, and mathematical concepts through activity-based learning.
Children develop problem-solving abilities, critical thinking, and emotional readiness.
The program also encourages independence, responsibility, and teamwork.
