Our Programs

Age-appropriate learning programs designed to nurture every childs growth.

Playgroup

(2 - 3 Years)

Our Playgroup program offers a gentle introduction to school life in a safe, caring, and nurturing environment, where children learn through play, music, movement, and joyful social interaction.

Early language development begins at this stage, with children introduced to alphabets, simple words, and basic sentence formation by connecting learning to their everyday surroundings.

Creative workshops such as pottery, lantern making, toy making, and other hands-on activities help build curiosity, confidence, and fine motor skills. The Matern Roots Language Module forms the foundation for all further learning programs.

Nursery

(3 - 4 Years)

Our Nursery program combines structured learning with creative play to support early literacy, numeracy, communication, and growing confidence.

Children explore learning through engaging activities, with a unique Science with Fun module that introduces basic concepts through simple stories and hands-on experiments.

The Nursery Math module gently introduces addition, subtraction, and multiplication in a playful and effortless way, while continued focus on language, creativity, and social interaction builds curiosity and independence.

UKG or Junior Kg Programme

(4 - 5 Years)

Our UKG or junior program supports a smooth transition into formal schooling through activity-based learning and structured guidance.

The curriculum is designed around five core modules - Science, Mathematics, Language Development, Environmental and Health Sciences, along with Art and Music.

Children explore math concepts like addition, subtraction, multiplication, geometry, and problem-solving in a hands-on way, while science learning includes simple experiments, observations, and early exposure to technology concepts.

Language development focuses on reading, writing, phonics, grammar, storytelling, public speaking, and introduction to a second language. Environmental learning builds awareness of nature, health, hygiene, and responsible living, while art and music nurture creativity, expression, and cultural appreciation.

The program encourages independent thinking, emotional readiness, teamwork, and confidence for primary school.
