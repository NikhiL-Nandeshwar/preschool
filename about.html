About Mattern Roots Preschool

A calm, caring space where children feel safe to grow.

Mattern Roots Preschool was created with one simple intention - to offer young children a peaceful, nurturing environment where they feel secure, respected, and understood.

Located in Kolhapur, our preschool focuses on early learning through play, routine, and gentle guidance. We believe children learn best when they are emotionally safe and personally cared for.

With small class sizes and attentive teachers, we take the time to understand each child and support their natural pace of growth.

Mattern Roots Preschool is a nurturing space where young minds grow through play, care, and curiosity. We focus on early learning that builds confidence, creativity, and strong values.
