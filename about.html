Our Story

Mattern Roots Preschool was created with one simple belief - early childhood should be joyful, safe, and meaningful.

Located in Kolhapur, we provide a nurturing space where children are encouraged to explore, express, and grow at their own pace, supported by caring teachers and thoughtful routines.
