About Matern Roots Preschool

A calm, caring space where children feel safe to grow.

Matern Roots Preschool was founded with a clear purpose - to create a nurturing learning environment where young children feel emotionally secure, respected, and encouraged to grow at their own pace.

Located in Kolhapur, our preschool focuses on early childhood learning through play, routine, exploration, and gentle guidance. We believe that strong emotional security forms the foundation for confident learning.

With small class sizes and attentive educators, we take the time to understand each child as an individual and support their natural curiosity, confidence, and development.

Matern Roots Experimental Lab Based Teaching Programme (ELTEP)

At Matern Roots Preschool, learning is guided by our Experimental Lab Based Teaching Programme (ELTEP). This approach introduces children to hands-on learning experiences that encourage observation, exploration, and logical thinking in an age-appropriate manner.

Through guided activities and simple experiments, children learn by doing rather than memorizing. This method helps develop early logic and reasoning abilities while keeping learning joyful, meaningful, and stress-free.

Global Standards and Finland Accreditation

Our curriculum, pedagogy, teaching methods, and teacher training practices are designed in alignment with the National Education Policy (NEP), focusing on the critical developmental years from birth to six years of age.

We follow a highly advanced academic framework inspired by global early education standards and are in the process of obtaining accreditation from Finland. This ongoing accreditation reflects our commitment to quality, research-based learning practices.

Alongside academics, creative and art-based activities are thoughtfully integrated to support emotional expression, social development, and confidence building in young children.
